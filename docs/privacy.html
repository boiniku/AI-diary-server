Privacy Policy

Established Date: December 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy establishes how user personal information is handled in the smartphone application "Kiku." (hereinafter referred to as "this App") provided by Noborida Kyotaro (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "us").

1. Information We Collect

This App may collect and use the following information to provide its services.

A. Information Provided by Users

  • Account Information: Username and password (stored encrypted).
  • Diary Data: Text data of diaries entered by users, emotion scores, and titles.
  • Image Data: Images uploaded by users to attach to their diaries.

B. Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: We may collect identifiers such as the device's Advertising ID (IDFA/AAID) for ad delivery purposes.
  • Usage Data: Usage history and log information of the App.

2. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  1. To provide and operate the service:
    • User authentication.
    • Saving and displaying diary data.
    • Providing AI-based conversation features (responses based on diary content, title generation, emotion analysis).
  2. To improve AI accuracy:
    • Text and images entered by users are processed via the OpenAI API.
  3. For ad delivery:
    • Displaying third-party ads using Google AdMob.

3. External Transmission and Third-Party Provision

This App uses the following third-party services, and some information is transmitted to them.

OpenAI API

  • Information Transmitted: Diary text entered by users, uploaded images.
  • Purpose: AI response generation, emotion analysis, title summarization.
  • Details: This App uses the API provided by OpenAI. Transmitted data is handled in accordance with OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

  • Information Transmitted: Advertising ID, device information, app usage data.
  • Purpose: To display appropriate ads to users (personalized ads).
  • Details: Google and its partners use cookies and device IDs to deliver ads.
  • Google Privacy & Terms

4. Data Management

Images and text data entrusted to us by users are stored on the servers within this App. Passwords are hashed and stored securely.

5. Encryption of Communication

Communication between this App and the server is protected using appropriate measures.

6. Suspension of Transmission and Deletion of User Information

Users can request the deletion of their information through the account deletion function of this App (if implemented) or by contacting us. Also, users can refuse the transmission of Advertising IDs (tracking) through their device settings.
